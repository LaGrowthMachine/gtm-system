Header KPIs

Reply-rate zones — based on La Growth Machine benchmark targets

On target — LinkedIn reply ≥25%, email reply ≥6%
Watch — LinkedIn 15-25%, email 3-6%. Optimization, not an alarm.
To fix — LinkedIn <15%, email <3%.
Low sample — under the 20-contact floor; rates aren't reliable yet.

The two table scopes

This week · active routine
Reply rate, acceptance & awaiting cover only the campaigns you're actively working — a rep can be 0% here if their active campaign is below the floor.
All-time · all campaigns
Outcome, conversion & value cover every campaign ever run, incl. retired — where older outcomes live. So 0% active reply with N all-time outcomes is expected, not a bug.
Conversion
Outcome ÷ contacted on the campaigns that produced it. * = estimated where retired-campaign volume is inferred.

Reply classification doctrine

🔥 Hot — explicit ask to move forward. Needs speed.
💬 Curious — engaged, no objection. Needs nurture-to-outcome.
🛑 Objection — pushes back with a reason. Needs handling.
🧰 Equipped — uses a competitor. Needs a differentiation angle.
🚫 Firm no — hard no / AI callout. Close cleanly, log why.
🎯 Wrong fit — not the ICP. A targeting signal.
